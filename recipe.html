Perfect Pot Roast Recipe

Ingredients

1 tsp ground black pepper
3-5 lbs chuck roast
2-3 tbsps olive oil
2 onions
6-8 carrots
1 cup red wine
3 cups beef broth
2-3 sprigs fresh rosemary
2-3 sprigs fresh thyme

Directions

  • Preheat the oven
  • Generously salt and pepper the chuck roast
  • Heat the olive oil in large pot over medium high heat. Add halved onions and brown. Remove onions.
  • Throw the carrots into the same pot and toss until browned. Reserve the carrots with the onions.
  • Add some more olive oil and place the meat in the pot and sear it for about a minute. Remove the roast to a plate
  • EAT!
